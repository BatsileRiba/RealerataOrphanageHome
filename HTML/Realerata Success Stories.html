Our Success Stories


This is how our journey has been going so far...

We received a donation of 30 companions (Labrador retriever puppies) which are now part of our family, taking care of us emotionally.

Over 10,000 clothing items have been donated to our home. Our children will always be clothed.

We have been awarded a certificate for the best orphanage home in Gauteng by the government.

One of our children won first place in the 2023 Olympics.

We are proud to share that among our 150 housed children, over 90 were found homeless on the streets and then offered a home.
