About Us


Founded in 2005, Realerata has been a home to over 150 loved children and adults. We take children off the streets and provide a supportive family environment where they can grow, learn, and thrive. We believe in discipline, equality, and providing every child with the chance to succeed. Our tutoring services and support ensure our children have the tools to attend top universities in South Africa.
